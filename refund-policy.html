Cancellation/Refund Policy

At FilesCAD, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, we offer a full refund within 7 days of your purchase date. 

Eligibility for a Refund
The refund request must be made within 7 days of the purchase date.
The product must be unused and in its original packaging.
The refund request must be accompanied by a valid proof of purchase (such as an order number or receipt).

How to Request a Refund
Contact our customer support team at info@filescad.com.
Provide your name, contact information, and proof of purchase.
Indicate the reason for your refund request.
Our customer support team will review your request and process your refund within 7-10 business days.

Exceptions 
We do not offer refunds for digital products, such as software downloads, after they have been downloaded or used. We reserve the right to refuse a refund request if it is deemed to be fraudulent or in violation of our terms and conditions.
