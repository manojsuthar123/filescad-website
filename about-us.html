About Us

FilesCAD provides vector, graphic, 2D and 3D artistic drawings consisting of various categories in this section. All of these designs are cuttable and printable designs. They can be cut with the Co2 laser, CNC router, water jet, plasma, cricut, scroll saw and vinyl cutting machines and printed on printers. Various materials such as wood, MDF, polywood, acrylic, balsa,vinyl, cardboard can be made into objects with these templates. These templates can be used in many areas such as decoration, stencil, wall art, Fabric print, damask, carving, engraving, sticker, T-shirt printing, silk screen, felt cut template, jewelry pattern, ornament, Vinyl Decals, clipart vector.
